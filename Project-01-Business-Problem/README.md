Project 01 - Business Problem Understanding

1. Company
**ShopSphere** (Fictional E-Commerce Company)

2. Business Problem

ShopSphere is an e-commerce company experiencing continuous sales growth, but its overall profitability remains inconsistent. The company wants to identify the reasons behind low-profit products, underperforming regions, and the impact of discounts on business performance.

---

3. Questions Data Should Answer

- Which product categories generate the highest sales?
- Which categories generate the highest profit?
- Which regions perform the best and worst?
- Which products are causing financial losses?
- How do discounts affect profitability?
- What are the monthly sales trends?

---

4. Business Goals

- Increase overall profitability.
- Improve sales performance across all regions.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Identify top-performing products and categories.
- Support data-driven business decisions.

---

5. Expected Analytical Output

- Clean and analysis-ready dataset.
- Sales performance dashboard.
- SQL-based business insights.
- Python data analysis.
- Interactive Power BI dashboard.
- Business recommendations based on analytical findings.

---

6. Tools Used

- Business Analysis
- Documentation
- Microsoft Excel
- SQL
- Python
- Power BI

# Project 07: EDA Insight Report

1. 📌 Objective

Perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Sample Superstore dataset to discover business trends, identify risks, and generate actionable business insights.

---

2. 📂 Dataset

- Dataset: Sample Superstore
- Total Records: 9,994
- Total Columns: 13

---

3. 🛠 Tools Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Google Colab

---

4. 📊 Analysis Performed

- Loaded the dataset
- Checked dataset information
- Verified missing values
- Identified duplicate records
- Generated summary statistics
- Region-wise Sales Analysis
- Category-wise Sales Analysis
- Region-wise Profit Analysis
- Category-wise Profit Analysis
- Segment-wise Sales Analysis
- Top 10 Sub-Categories by Sales
- Created business visualizations using bar charts

---

5. 📈 Key Business Insights

- West Region generated the highest sales and overall profit.
- Technology was the most profitable product category.
- Consumer Segment contributed the highest overall sales.
- Phones and Chairs were the top-selling sub-categories.
- Furniture generated comparatively lower profit than Technology and Office Supplies.

7. 🎯 Conclusion

This project demonstrates how Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) can transform raw business data into meaningful insights. The findings can help businesses identify high-performing regions, profitable product categories, customer segments, and potential opportunities for improving business performance.

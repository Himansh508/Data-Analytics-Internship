# 📊 Project 08: BI Dashboard Project

1. 🎯 Objective

The objective of this project is to design an interactive Business Intelligence Dashboard using the Sample Superstore dataset. The dashboard helps visualize key business KPIs and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

2. 🛠 Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- Sample Superstore Dataset

---

3. 📁 Dataset

- Rows: 9,994
- Columns: 13

---

4. 📈 Dashboard Visuals

The dashboard contains the following visuals:

- Sales by Region
- Profit by Region
- Sales by Category
- Sales by Segment (Donut Chart)

---

5. 💡 Key Insights

- West Region generated the highest sales.
- West Region also achieved the highest profit.
- Technology is the highest-performing product category.
- Consumer Segment contributes the largest share of total sales.
- Central and South regions have lower performance and provide opportunities for business improvement.

---

6. 📊 Business Recommendations

- Increase investment in the West and East regions.
- Improve marketing strategies in Central and South regions.
- Focus on Technology products to maximize revenue.
- Create targeted campaigns for Corporate and Home Office customers.
